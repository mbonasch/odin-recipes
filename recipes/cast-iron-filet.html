Cast Iron Filet Mignon



Filet mignon is a tender cut from the tip of the tendferloin that includes tight marbeling and rich flavor.


Ingredients


Makes enough for 1 serving
  • 1 8oz Beef Filet Mignon
  • 1 tbs salted butter
  • Salt/Pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Remove steaks from fridge 45 mins to 1 hr before cooking
    to allow steaks to come to room temperature
  2. Season both sides with salt and pepper after removing
    from fridge to allow seasoning to settle into meat
  3. pre-heat oven to 450 degrees
  4. Heat a cast iron skillet to smoking
  5. Cook steaks for 2 minutes per side or until sear has developed
  6. place steaks on cast iron in oven for 7-9 minutes
    or until 145 internal temp
