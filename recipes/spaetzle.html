Spaetzle



Spaetzle is a traditional german noodle made from flour, eggs, water, and a little bit of oil.


Ingredients


Makes enough for 5 servings
  • 1 spaetzle press
  • 1 strainer spoon
  • Flour (5 cups)
  • Eggs (5)
  • Water (5tbs)
  • Oil (neutral) (5 tbs)

Directions

  1. boil 1 qt of water in large pot
  2. dump 5 cups of flour into large bowl
  3. crack 5 eggs into large bowl
  4. add 5 tbs oil
  5. add 5 tbs water
  6. stir until dough-like consistency
  7. place dough into Spaetzle press
  8. squeeze into boiling water
  9. as noodles begin to float, pull out of water with strainer spoon
  10. repeat steps 7 through 9 until all dough has been used
  11. serve fresh
